About Us

THE ACHIEVERS

THE ACHIEVERS is a team which provide the support and development in each and every dimension of IT sector. The vision of this group is to make every technocrat, a good leader and entrepreneur by providing the hands-on experience on the live project like software development to individual and software company and many more......

WHY ACHIEVERS

 The Achievers will be making you future ready by learning and dealing with the various Technical skills like web development, app development, game development, software development AI & ML as well as AR and VR and work as a Software company.

VISION AND MISSION

 To achieve excellence in every sphere and make every one self-reliant with high-spirited personality and  identify and remove all the crisis and enhance the capabilities of the tech-savvy students. 

FUTURE PLANS

To improve the concept of Earn "While You Learn" .

  • From learning skills and then implementing them on live projects and then earning through various platform, making students self dependent and self reliant.

Implementation of cutting-edge technical skills and using them in live projects.

  • Working on cutting edge skills making the students a completely ready for real life work situation.

Conducting workshops/seminars and live lectures in schools and other institution.

  • Conducting live workshops in various institutes, let them know about the projects we have done and giving them knowledge about various fields of science & giving them a roadmap for the future. 

To inculcate entrepreneurship ideology and business ethics

               Developing a entrepreneurship mindset in every student .
